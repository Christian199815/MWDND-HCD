How to use:

1. Click keys to select them in the order you want to type

2. As you select keys, word suggestions will appear

3. The green suggestion shows the direct word formed by your key sequence

4. Click a suggestion to add it to your text

5. Use "Backspace Key" to remove the last selected key

6. Use "Clear Keys" to clear all selected keys

7. Use "Delete Word" to remove the last word from your text

8. Switch to "Full Alphabet" to directly type individual letters
